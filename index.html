Hello! My name is Donghyun Kim, and I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science.
Currently, I am working at the Internet Computing Lab. (ICL), supervised by Prof. Kyong-Ho Lee.
My research focuses on advancing knowledge representation and reasoning techniques for cognitive AI systems.

Specifically, I am interested in:
1) Knowledge Extraction and Validation
2) Human-like Conversational AI with Knowledge Reasoning
3) Synergizing Large Language Models and Knowledge Graphs
